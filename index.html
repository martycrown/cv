Singer and songwriter

Marty Crown is a San Francisco based singer and songwriter. Her second EP “Hologram” released in the Spring of 2017 аnd soon she is going to release her new breathtaking EP. Already a sensation in her native Kazakhstan, Marty was the winner of Samsung’s EDGE Fest and has been featured in ELLE, Cosmopolitan, Harper’s Bazaar, Esquire, Boulevard, SNC, L’Officiel magazines. She moved to the U.S. in 2016 to further her ethno-electronic career under Silver Gun Records. Often compared to the likes of Björk, Grimes and FKA Twigs, Marty infuses her classic opera training with offbeat ethno-pop sensibilities.
